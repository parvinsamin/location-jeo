HUG’s Three Innovative Ideas

#1 Attach Content to Locations

People are always moving—passing by businesses, traveling through neighborhoods, visiting museums, going to class, etc.

With HUG, you attach your content to any location you like. People passing by that location (from 50 feet up to a 5-mile radius) will receive your HUG on their mobile devices based on their interests.

#2 Match Content to Individualized Interests

Like a dating site, HUG users list their specific interests and only receive content that matches those interests. No need to build a following, earn likes, or bother anyone with unwanted posts.

#3 Bring Location and Interests Together

HUG recognizes that the places we are often reflect our personal interests, even if it’s the grocery store. By bringing interests and locations together, HUG helps people make intuitive, instant connections within a deliverable proximity.

Receiving the store coupons right as you approach the store could be very handy. If you like coupons, that is!

The Benefits

The biggest benefit of HUG is reaching like-minded people where they are at the exact time they are interested in what you have to say. Here’s what we mean:

HUG Increases Walk-in Traffic for Your Local Business

#1 HUG Story

Bob likes coffee...a lot! He adds "espresso drinks" to his list of interests in his HUG account. Passing through a new neighborhood, Bob gets a HUG on his phone from a local coffee shop describing their double espresso peanut butter latte. Bob can’t resist and stops in.

By using HUG, that coffee shop increases walk-in sales by reaching new customers as they pass through town.

HUG Connects You with Like-Minded People All Over the World

HUG Connects You with Like-Minded People All Over the World

#2 HUG Story

Sheila’s into folk music. Unfortunately, The Folk Music Hall of Fame in Beacon, NY is too far for her to travel to. But with a HUG account, the Hall of Fame can reach over the miles and attach HUGs to any location ever mentioned in a folk song. When Sheila or anyone passes by one of these spots anywhere in the country, they get a HUG with an audio recording of the song—and a link to a Hall of Fame t-shirt!

Because HUG subscribers can reach like-minded people anywhere in the world by placing HUGs in locations of shared interest—a wider community of like-minded people is born and information is shared more organically.

HUG Reaches People at the Exact Right Times

#3 HUG Story

Felicia owns an all-night emergency taco business super popular with college students. Every night from 10:30 pm to 4 am, a HUG from Felicia goes out to anyone on two local campuses interested in "tacos" or "late-night delivery". Her HUG can be anything from coupons and recipes to "notes from the all-night taco girl"-no need to pass out fliers, build a following online, or even use radio ads.

With HUG, you can reach the right people any time of day or night, whether you set up a HUG to last an hour, a week, or years.

HUG Saves Time When It Matters

#4 HUG Story

Juan lives near a traffic light in a heavy traffic area. One afternoon, the light stops working and two cars collide, making it impossible for others to pass. Juan attaches a HUG to the traffic light two blocks away, warning incoming travelers interested in "traffic updates" to take an alternate route.

HUG delivers news quickly in emergencies or just when plans have changed. Last-minute cancellations, traffic reports, or weather notices go out to the people who need them in real time.
